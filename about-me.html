About Me

Sydney is a student at Miami University in Oxford, Ohio. She is majoring
in Journalism and Emerging Technology in Business and Design. She has
four years of experienece designing and writing for journalism programs.
In the future she wants to work in the creative advertising field.

Sydney currently works with the Miami Student. Here she designs graph-
ics, writes for the Campus and Community and Culture sections, and set-
s up page designs. Last semester, she was at the University of St. Thomas
where she designed for the Crest. Sydney also designed and wrote for T-
he Messenger, her high school's news magazine.

She has experience with:

  • Illustrator
  • InDesign
  • Photoshop
  • Lightroom
