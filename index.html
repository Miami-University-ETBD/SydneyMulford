Sydney's History in Graphic Design

Sydney started leaning Graphic Design sophomore year of high school.
She started on Illustrator and quickly fell in love. Since then, she has
furthered her skills and learned Photoshop, InDesign, and Lightroom.
While Sydney loves creating vector-based works in Illustrator, she is
always ready to learn different platforms and new techniques.
