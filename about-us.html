About Us


We are so glad you found us!

We are a nonprofit pet adoption organizations started in Oxford, Ohio. Paws
for Paws was started by two college students at Miami University looking fo-
r ways to use their business degrees. We focus on offering the highest qual-
ity care while our pets are in our possession, but also trying to get you
all to adopt! We host various events in Oxford to show off our fabulous p-
ets! Check out our event schedule below!
